We are all worthy of a pedestal

And all to life we should be true

For our soul to grow richer

We must work harder for our due

<br>

The testament was made to sow

Boil blood in veins, bind in heat

Of future that we cannot know

It tells us what it can of it

<br>

After all tulips sprout from seed

All life force reaching for the light

And suddenly one day, unknowing

They shoot upward with pistol's might


<br>


Our life it never stops or tires

Of changing its own step and form

When the end comes, a dream of pyres

In mother's embrace we're reborn


<br>


And I can say it doesn't suit me

To chase cheap thrills til Ioss of sight

Deserve a pedestal are they

Who tirelessly walk towards the light


<br>

#### Ivan Bagaev
##### Translated by Andrew Hollis
