The colour of your love comes from dreams

From a distant likeness you appear

With a hint of smile on your lips

As always, waiting for me here

<br>

Your beauty's landscape frames an evening

Painting of fire in the sky, I'm struck

Just like in waiting this moment is eternal, fleeting

No roads to it ever lead back

<br>

The colour of your love comes from dreams

An everlasting light in me

Within our never aging hearts

Eternal youth will always be

<br>

#### Ivan Bagaev
##### Translated by Andrew Hollis
