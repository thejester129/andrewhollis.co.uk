Let's love but never burn with love

Tame nor thoughts nor human will

To be wholeheartedly, decidedly alive

And fully give yourself to happiness and ill

<br>

And in the deepest corners of the soul

And to a meeting with all future lovers

You, born in silence, never growing old

I carry with me hidden under covers

<br>

#### Ivan Bagaev
##### Translated by Andrew Hollis
